About Us

WIN provides networking events, mentorship opportunities, and other programming to connect, empower, and promote women in the investment industry. The organization currently serves the Central Indiana area.

  • Learn More

Events

WIN hosts networking events in the Central Indiana area. Events vary in formality and often feature speakers. All are invited to register for and attend events.

  • View Our Upcoming Events

Mentorship Program

The WIN mentorship program aims to connect women who are early in their investment careers with experienced investment professionals. Members of all levels of experience and backgrounds, including undergraduates, are welcome to apply to be a mentor or mentee.

  • Interested?

Membership

Members receive access to early event registration, members-only events, and the mentorship program. Both women and men of all levels of investment experience and backgrounds, including undergraduates, are welcome to become members. The standard annual membership fee is $100, or $25 for students.

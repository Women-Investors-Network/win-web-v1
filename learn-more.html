Women Investors Network

Our Mission

WIN seeks to connect, empower, and promote women in the investment industry.

  • Connect

    WIN provides networking events to connect women of all levels of experience and backgrounds within in the investment industry. Events are primarily held in the Central Indiana area.

  • Empower

    The WIN mentorship program seeks to pair women who are early in their investment careers with experienced investment professionals. In addition, WIN plans to collaborate with local universities to educate undergraduate women on the variety of investment career paths and resources available to them.

  • Promote

    Potential future initiatives include career services, featuring local women investment professionals on our website, and a scholarship opportunity for undergraduates.

History

When founder Sara Omohundro realized that no organization for women in the investment industry existed in Central Indiana, she sought to change that. Sara understood the positive impact that having female connections, mentors, and role-models within the industry could have on her own career and knew that other women could benefit from making those connections as well. In 2019, WIN was organized to fill that need. Although WIN is a young organization, it seeks to host a variety of events and programming in the near future. As the organization grows, it hopes to expand its reach beyond the Indianapolis area.

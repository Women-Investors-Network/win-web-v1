Benefits

Members gain access to a series of benefits within the Women Investors Network.
  • Receive discounted registration for all WIN events
  • Gain access to exclusive members-only events
  • Become a mentor or mentee through the WIN Mentorship Program
  • Individual | $80/year

Student

Active University students may receive a discount on their Women Investors Network membership.

  • Students | $20/year

Corporate

Organizations may purchase a bulk membership for all individuals employed by the organization, granting them access to Women Investors Network events and benefits.

  • Corporate | $400/year
